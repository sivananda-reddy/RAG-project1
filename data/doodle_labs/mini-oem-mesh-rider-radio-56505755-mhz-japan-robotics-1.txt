RM-5700-2LSX-SA-ST
Japan Robotics
The Doodle Labs mini -OEM and Wearable V2 Mesh Rider Radios feature a specialty Japan Robotics band . It is important to note, for the mini -OEM there is custom Firmware and different connector styles than the 2023 Update. These changes render the RM-5700-2L incompatible with the 2023 Update and only RM-5700-2L firmware should not be used for this model.
Change List
1. Primary connector replaced by 2 connectors, with 1 connector for hardware interfaces and 1 connector for additional power supply
2. Hardware interface connector (
SM15B) + additional power supply connector (SM6B)
3. Custom Firmware: RM-5700 Release
